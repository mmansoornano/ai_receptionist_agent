=== Frequently Asked Questions ===
Q: Do you offer virtual appointments?
A: Yes, we offer virtual consultations via video call. Please specify when booking.

Q: What should I bring to my appointment?
A: Please bring a valid ID, insurance card (if applicable), and any relevant medical records.

Q: Is parking available?
A: Yes, free parking is available in the lot behind the building.

Q: Do you have wheelchair access?
A: Yes, our facility is fully accessible with wheelchair ramps and accessible restrooms.

=== Special Accommodations ===
We offer services in multiple languages. Please inform us when booking if you need translation services.

For hearing-impaired customers, we provide sign language interpreters with 48-hour advance notice.

=== Refund Policy ===
- Full refund: Cancellations made 24+ hours in advance
- 50% refund: Cancellations made 4-24 hours in advance
- No refund: Cancellations made less than 4 hours in advance or no-shows
